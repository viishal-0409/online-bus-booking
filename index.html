Our Story

Clothes that start with the fabric, not the trend.

Vastra began as a small tailoring table and a simple rule: choose the cloth first, let the cut follow. Every kurti and trouser in this collection is finished by hand in small batches, so quality stays consistent even as the catalogue grows.

No rush deliveries, no mystery fabric blends — just clothes meant to be worn often and last.
